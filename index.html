Hi, I'm Jess.

WHO I AM

I'm a UK based junior full-stack developer, passionate about code, architecture, technology, science and mathematics. I like helping others, playing games, reading and all things astrophysics.


WHAT I DO

I work on large-scale web applications, I create websites, I build small, random, useful applications. I work on algorithms to assist with astrophysics research. I mess around with a variety of languages and technologies just because it's fun and I love to learn.


WORK

I work with a wonderful team on a large-scale web application for HR and payroll within the UK, at CascadeHR.


HOBBIES

I play games, I read, I code. I like to walk - especially with my doggy Lucky. I care greatly about others and I like to volunteer. I also hope to soon begin German language classes.
